About Us

ABA Heating and Cooling

About Us Established in 1994, ABA Heating and Cooling is a family-owned and operated HVAC company serving the Greater Austin area. With over 30 years of experience, we have completed more than 10,000 air conditioning installations and served over 65,000 satisfied customers throughout Central Texas. Our mission is to provide the best service at the best price, with customer satisfaction as our top priority. Led by President Randy Jenkins, our team is committed to honest, ethical, and reliable service. We employ NATE-certified technicians and knowledgeable staff who are dedicated to addressing your heating and cooling needs efficiently and professionally. As a fully licensed and insured contractor (License #TACLB017901E), we ensure peace of mind with every service we provide. Business Description ABA Heating and Cooling offers comprehensive HVAC services to both residential and commercial clients in Austin and the surrounding areas. Our services include: • Air Conditioning Services: Expert repair, installation, and maintenance of central air systems, ductless mini-splits, and packaged units. • Heating Services: Professional furnace and heat pump repair, installation, and maintenance to keep your home warm during cooler months. • Emergency HVAC Services: 24/7 emergency repair services to address urgent heating and cooling issues promptly. • Commercial HVAC Services: Tailored solutions for businesses, including repair, replacement, installation, and maintenance of commercial HVAC systems. • Preventative Maintenance Plans: Comprehensive maintenance programs featuring 15-point AC and 26-point heating tune-ups to ensure system efficiency and longevity. We proudly serve a wide range of communities in the Greater Austin area, including Cedar Park, Round Rock, Pflugerville, Lakeway, Leander, Georgetown, Kyle, Bee Cave, Lago Vista, Buda, Liberty Hill, and Manchaca. At ABA Heating and Cooling, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality HVAC solutions with a focus on customer satisfaction. Whether you need routine maintenance or emergency repairs, our team is here to ensure your comfort year-round.
